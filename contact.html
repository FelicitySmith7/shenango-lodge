Frequently Asked Questions

Is there cell service at the lodge? ▼

No, the lodge is completely off-grid with no cell service, which is part of its charm. This disconnection from technology allows you to fully immerse yourself in nature and enjoy quality time with family and friends.

What should I bring? ▼

Pack clothing suitable for all weather conditions, as northern Ontario weather can change quickly. Bring your personal fishing and hunting gear if you have preferred equipment. All bedding and linens are provided. Contact us for a detailed packing list.
