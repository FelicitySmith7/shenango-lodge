Wilderness Awaits

Discover Your Adventure

Fishing

Fish Lake Shenango for walleye, northern pike, and perch. Our waters offer excellent angling opportunities in a remote wilderness setting.

What We Provide

Boats, motors, and fishing gear provided for guests. Contact us for details.

Wilderness Exploration

The lodge sits on a peninsula in Lake Shenango, accessible by seaplane. Explore the surrounding wilderness and experience the tranquility of a remote location far from civilization.

Nature & Wildlife

Being remote, Shenango offers opportunities to observe wildlife in their natural habitat. Clear nights provide excellent stargazing far from light pollution.

Sauna & Relaxation

Relax after a day of activities in our sauna and shower building. The 12x23 facility provides a place to unwind and refresh after time on the water.

What We Offer

  • Sauna and shower building
  • Available daily for all guests

Hunting

Shenango is a remote hunting lodge. Hunting is available during Ontario's hunting seasons.

Requirements

  • Valid hunting license
  • Advance booking essential
  • All provincial hunting regulations must be followed
