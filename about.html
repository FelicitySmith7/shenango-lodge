About Us

Fishing & Hunting Lodge — Lake Shenango, Ontario

The Lodge

Shenango Lodge is a fishing and hunting lodge on a peninsula in Lake Shenango, near Foleyet, Ontario. The lodge is remote, off-grid, solar powered, and accessible by seaplane.

Quick Facts

Off-Grid & Solar Powered

Each cabin runs on its own solar system with solar lights, a solar fridge, and a propane cook stove. Steel roofs on every building.

Four Cabins

Four guest cabins on the peninsula, plus a log cabin lodge, sauna and shower building, and storage buildings.

Fishing & Hunting

Walleye and northern pike fishing. Hunting opportunities throughout the seasons. Contact us for details.

Location

Lake Shenango, near Foleyet, Northern Ontario. Mailing address: Box 66, Foleyet, ON, Canada, P0M 1T0.
