Your Home on the Water

Four Cabins. One Peninsula. Infinite Possibilities.

Lakeside Sanctuaries

Four cabins on a peninsula in Lake Shenango. Each cabin runs on its own solar system with solar lights and a solar fridge, plus a propane cook stove. All buildings have steel roofs.
