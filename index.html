Shenango Lodge is a remote fishing and hunting lodge on a peninsula in Lake Shenango, near Foleyet, Ontario. Off-grid and solar powered, accessible by seaplane.

Shenango Lodge sits on a peninsula in Lake Shenango, near Foleyet in Northern Ontario. The lodge is completely off-grid — solar powered with four guest cabins.

Walleye and northern pike fishing. Hunting opportunities in season. The lodge provides everything you need for your stay.

Your Home on the Water

Four cabins on the peninsula, each with its own character and view. Solar-powered, wood-stove heated, and built to weather the Ontario seasons. Every cabin has lake access, propane kitchen, and bedding provided. Shared sauna and showers at the main lodge.

Fishing

Walleye and northern pike. The lake is generous with those who know where to look. Guides available.

Canoeing

Several lakes to paddle and explore. Each one different. Pack a lunch and spend the day out there.

Wildlife

Moose, bears, eagles, loons — they're all out here. Dawn and dusk are your best bet for spotting them.

Hiking

Trails through old-growth forest. Plenty to explore on foot around the peninsula and beyond.

Sauna & Swim

Wind down after a long day. Our 12x23 sauna and shower building is available for all guests.

Stargazing

No light pollution. Zero light pollution out here. You won't believe the sky.
